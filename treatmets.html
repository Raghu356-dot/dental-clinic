Our Treatments

Teeth Cleaning & Scaling

We offer advanced gum care using LASER technology for precise and effective treatment. Scaling removes plaque and tartar buildup, restoring gum health and preventing disease.

Tooth Filling

Our expert tooth filling services repair cavities, trauma, or wear with high-quality materials to preserve natural teeth and prevent further decay.

Tooth Extraction

When a tooth is beyond repair, we perform extractions using gentle techniques to minimize discomfort and ensure a smooth recovery process.

Artificial Complete Denture

We craft high-quality dentures designed with precision for a natural look, restoring both function and aesthetics for missing teeth.

Dental Implants

Using titanium-based implants, we replace missing teeth with durable, natural-looking prosthetics that restore full oral function and confidence.

Laser Dentistry

Our advanced laser technology enables precise, minimally invasive treatments with faster healing, reduced discomfort, and improved safety.

Root Canal Therapy

We remove infected pulp, clean thoroughly, and seal the tooth to relieve pain, eliminate infection, and preserve natural tooth structure.

Wisdom Tooth Extraction

Gentle, effective removal of impacted or problematic wisdom teeth with anesthesia options for a pain-free, stress-free experience.

Fixed Partial Denture

Custom-made dental bridges restore chewing function, stability, and a beautiful smile by anchoring prosthetics to adjacent teeth.

Teeth Whitening

Safe and effective bleaching treatments remove stains and discoloration, restoring your smile’s natural radiance and brightness.

Veneers

Custom-made veneers cover imperfections like chips, gaps, and discoloration, transforming your smile with a flawless finish.

Pediatric Dentistry

Gentle and comprehensive care for children, focusing on preventive treatments, comfort, and healthy dental habits for life.

Flap Surgery

Advanced gum surgery for severe periodontal disease, removing deep plaque and bacteria to restore gum health and prevent further issues.

Orthodontic Braces

We provide customized braces (metal, ceramic, or lingual) to correct misaligned teeth, improving bite and creating a confident smile.

Clear Aligners

Discreet, removable trays gradually shift teeth into place, offering comfort and flexibility as an alternative to traditional braces.
